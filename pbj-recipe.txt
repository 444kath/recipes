Ingredients:
	- 2 slices of bread
	- Peanut butter
	- Jelly
	
Directions:
1. Using a knife, spread peanut butter onto one side of one slice of bread.

2. Using a knife, spread jelly onto one side of the other slice of bread.

3. Put the two slices of bread together with the peanut butter and jelly facing inward toward each other.

4. Optional: Cut the sandwich diagonally into two triangular halves.
